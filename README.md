# Superstore-Sales-Profit-Analysis-Dashboard-Using-Tableau
Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze Superstore sales and profit performance across different regions, categories, segments, and shipping modes. The dashboard provides key business insights through KPIs, regional comparisons, and monthly trend analysis.

Key Features:
Total Sales, Average Sales, Total Profit, and Average Profit KPIs
Region-wise Sales and Profit analysis
Monthly Sales and Profit trend visualization
Interactive filters for Ship Mode, Category, and Segment
Performance comparison across regions
Dynamic dashboard for business decision-making
Tools Used: Tableau
Skills Demonstrated: Data Visualization, Dashboard Design, KPI Analysis, Business Intelligence, Data Analysis, Reporting
Project Outcome:
Enabled quick identification of top-performing regions, profit trends, and sales patterns, helping stakeholders make data-driven business decisions.
